# Steps Synthesis

AddCorrosive
en
Transfer corrosive reagent {reagent} ({volume}) to {vessel}.
zh
将腐蚀性试剂{reagent} ({volume})转移至{vessel}.

Example: Transfer corrosive reagent fuming nitric acid (50 mL) to flask.

Add (volume)
en
Add {reagent} ({volume}) to {vessel}.
zh
加入 {reagent}({volume}) 至{vessel}.

Example: Add MeCN (5 mL) to flask.

Add (mass)
en
Add {reagent} ({mass}) to {vessel}.
zh
加入{reagent} ({mass}) 至{vessel}.

Example: Add NaOH (0.5 g) to flask.

CleanVessel
en
Clean {vessel} with {solvent} ({cleans} × {volume}).
zh
用 {solvent} ({cleans} × {volume})洗涤 {vessel}.

Example: Clean flask with ether (2 × 50 mL).

Dissolve
en
Dissolve contents of {vessel} in {solvent} ({volume}) at {temp} over {time}.
zh
用{solvent}({volume}溶解{vessel} 中的组分，温度为{temp}时间为{time}。

Example: Dissolve contents of flask in DCM (50 mL) at 80°C over 10 minutes.

Dry
en
Dry contents of {vessel} for {time}.
zh
干燥{vessel}中的组分，时间为{time}

Example: Dry contents of flask for 60 seconds.

FilterThrough (not eluting)
en
Filter contents of {from_vessel} through {through_cartridge} into {to_vessel}.
zh
过滤{from_vessel}中的组分，经{through_cartridge}进入{to_vessel}.

Example: Filter contents of flask through drying cartridge into rotavap.

FilterThrough (eluting)
en
Filter contents of {from_vessel} through {through_cartridge} into {to_vessel}, eluting with {eluting_repeats} x {eluting_volume} of {eluting_solvent}.
zh
过滤{from_vessel}中的滤液经{through_cartridge}进入{to_vessel}, 用{eluting_solvent}{eluting_repeats}x{eluting_volume} 进行洗脱.

Example: Filter contents of flask through drying cartridge into rotavap, eluting with 3 x 50 mL of DCM.

Filter
en
Filter contents of {filter_vessel}.
zh
过滤{filter_vessel}中的组分。

Example: Filter contents of filter flask.

HeatChill
en
Heat/Chill {vessel} to {temp} for {time}.
zh
加热/冷却{vessel}至温度为{temp} 保持时间{time}.

Example: Heat/Chill flask to 80°C for 1 hr.

Precipitate
en
Trigger precipitation in {vessel} by chilling to {temp} for {time}.
zh
降温至温度为{temp}， 保持时间为{time}，促使{vessel}中的沉淀析出。

Example: Trigger precipitation in flask by chilling to -20°C for 1 hr.

Evaporate
en
Evaporate contents of {rotavap_name} at {temp} and {pressure} for {time}.
zh
在温度为{temp} 压力为{pressure}条件，下保持时间为{time}，蒸发除掉{rotavap_name} 中的成分。

Example: Evaporate contents of rotavap at 50°C and 70 mbar for 10 minutes.

Separate (wash)
en
Wash contents of {from_vessel} with {solvent} ({n_separations} x {solvent_volume}). Transfer waste phase ({waste_phase}) to {waste_phase_to_vessel} and product phase ({product_phase}) to {to_vessel}.
zh
用{solvent} ({n_separations} x {solvent_volume})洗涤{from_vessel}中组分. 转移废液相 ({waste_phase}) 至{waste_phase_to_vessel} ， 转移产品相({product_phase}) 至{to_vessel}.

Example: Wash contents of flask with ether (2 x 50 mL). Transfer waste phase (top) to round bottomed flask and product phase (bottom) to filter flask.

Separate (extract)
en
Extract contents of {from_vessel} with {solvent} ({n_separations} x {solvent_volume}). Transfer waste phase ({waste_phase}) to {waste_phase_to_vessel} and product phase ({product_phase}) to {to_vessel}.
zh
用{solvent} ({n_separations} x {solvent_volume})萃取 {from_vessel}中的组分. 转移废液相 ({waste_phase}) 至废液瓶{waste_phase_to_vessel} ， 转移产品相({product_phase}) 至容器{to_vessel}.

Example: Extract contents of flask with ether (2 x 50 mL). Transfer waste phase (top) to round bottomed flask and product phase (bottom) to filter flask.

WashSolid
en
Wash solid in {vessel} with {solvent} ({volume}).
zh
用{solvent} ({volume})洗涤{vessel}中的固体

Example: Wash solid in filter flask with DCM (50 mL).
